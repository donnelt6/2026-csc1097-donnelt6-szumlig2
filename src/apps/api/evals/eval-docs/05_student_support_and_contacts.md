# Student Support and Contacts

## Student Services
- Student Services desk location: Student Centre reception
- Opening hours: Monday to Friday, 09:00 to 17:00
- Email: studentservices@northbridge.example

## IT Help Desk
- Location: Library ground floor help point
- Opening hours: Monday to Friday, 08:30 to 18:00
- Email: ithelp@northbridge.example
- Phone: +353 1 555 0180

## Wellbeing service
- Confidential wellbeing appointments can be booked through the student portal.
- The wellbeing team runs a drop-in session every Thursday from 13:00 to 15:00 in Student Centre Room SC-12.
- Urgent welfare concerns should be escalated to the on-duty student support officer through reception.

## Disability support
- Students requiring academic accommodations should register with Disability Support as early as possible.
- The disability adviser clinic takes place on Tuesdays from 11:00 to 13:00 in SC-14.

## Password and access issues
- Students who cannot access email or the VLE should contact the IT Help Desk first.
- MFA reset requests are handled by the IT Help Desk, not the module team.

## Orientation support
- Orientation queries should be sent to orientation@northbridge.example.
- Students who miss mandatory orientation must contact the orientation team before the published deadline in the orientation guide.
