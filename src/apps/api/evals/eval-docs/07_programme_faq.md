# Programme FAQ

## When do classes begin after orientation?
Teaching begins on Monday 21 September 2026.

## Do I need to attend the orientation welcome session?
Yes. New students are expected to attend the mandatory welcome session unless Student Services has approved an exemption.

## Where should I go for help if I cannot log in to my email?
Contact the IT Help Desk. Password and MFA access problems are handled there.

## How late can coursework be before I receive zero?
More than 24 hours late, unless an approved extension applies.

## What format should CSC4042 coursework submissions use?
They should be uploaded through the VLE as a single ZIP archive named `studentnumber_csc4042.zip`.

## Are CSC4042 labs compulsory?
They are strongly recommended, but they are not graded separately.

## What room is used for the Wednesday setup clinic during orientation week?
The Wednesday VLE and email setup clinic takes place in TL-102.

## Who should I contact about orientation absences?
Email orientation@northbridge.example before the stated deadline.
