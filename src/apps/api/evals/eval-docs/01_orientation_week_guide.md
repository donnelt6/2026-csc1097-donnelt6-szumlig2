# Orientation Week Guide 2026

## Purpose
This guide explains the required orientation activities for first-year Computer Science students joining Northbridge Institute for the Autumn 2026 intake.

## Key dates
- Orientation week runs from Monday 14 September 2026 to Friday 18 September 2026.
- The mandatory in-person welcome session takes place on Monday 14 September 2026 from 09:30 to 11:00.
- Check-in opens at 08:45 on Monday 14 September 2026.
- The final deadline to complete online pre-arrival registration is Thursday 10 September 2026 at 17:00.

## Welcome session
- Location: Innovation Hall, Room IH-201
- Students must bring:
  - student ID or photo ID
  - laptop
  - proof of fee payment or sponsorship confirmation
- Attendance at the welcome session is mandatory for all new students unless an exemption has been approved by Student Services.

## Timetable for the week
- Monday 14 September
  - 09:30 to 11:00: Welcome session in IH-201
  - 11:15 to 12:00: Programme briefing in IH-204
  - 13:00 to 14:30: Campus systems setup lab in Tech Lab TL-102
- Tuesday 15 September
  - 10:00 to 12:00: Library induction in Learning Commons LC-1
  - 14:00 to 15:30: Small-group advisor meetings
- Wednesday 16 September
  - 09:00 to 10:30: Academic integrity workshop in IH-110
  - 11:00 to 12:30: VLE and email setup clinic in TL-102
- Thursday 17 September
  - 10:00 to 11:30: Wellbeing and support services talk in Student Centre SC-3
  - 13:00 to 14:00: Optional societies fair
- Friday 18 September
  - 09:30 to 11:00: Study skills session in LC-2
  - 11:15 to 12:00: Orientation wrap-up

## Systems setup requirements
- Students must activate their institutional email before attending the Wednesday setup clinic.
- Multi-factor authentication must be enabled by Friday 18 September 2026 at 12:00.
- Access to the programming lab environment is granted after the systems setup lab on Monday afternoon.

## Late arrival process
- Students who cannot attend on Monday must email orientation@northbridge.example before Friday 11 September 2026 at 12:00.
- Late arrivals are assigned a catch-up session on Wednesday 16 September 2026 at 15:00 in TL-103.

## Contact
- Orientation contact: orientation@northbridge.example
- Student Services desk: Student Centre reception, weekdays 09:00 to 17:00
